Welcome to Elementranquil

Elementranquil is a collection of relaxing animations and activities based on the five elements of earth, air, water, fire, and spirit.

This is a capstone project of a Galvanize student. As more modules are created, they will be added.

It is also a passion project of a person who has daily anxiety.
